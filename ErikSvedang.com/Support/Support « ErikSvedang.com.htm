Support

Ask questions in the form below and customer service will anser them asap.

System requirements

Operating System: Microsoft® Windows® XP / Vista

Additional Requirements: NET 2.0 Framework, XNA 3.0 support

Processor: 2.0 GHz Dual Core

Memory: 1 GB RAM

Hard Disk Space: 40 MB

Video Card: DirectX® 9.0c compatible video card, Shader Model 3.0 required. 256 MB

Sound Card: DirectX® 9.0c compatible sound card

DirectX® Version: DirectX® 9.0c

Why are the system requirements so high, it’s a simple 2d platformer!

That’s mainly because of the ecosystem simulation, the whole game world is simulated at all times. It’s a central part of what I wanted to explore with the game and I can’t optimize much more.

Things are just weird when I start the game!

Try installing the XNA 3.0 redist (it’s small and lightweight)

There is no wind or background music!

Seems like updating the drivers for your sound card will solve this. Please give it a try!

What about gamepad support?!

Good point. Luckily for you I plan to do a patch later when things have settled down, hopefully I can hack it in.

I want some other setting for the screen resolution!

I’m working on that. You can also try to modify the file “Settings.xml” in the Content folder of the game.
